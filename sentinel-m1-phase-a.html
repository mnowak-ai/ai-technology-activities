AYour shift

This part is all you

You're a Level 1 Security Monitoring Analyst. Today's job is to read the daily report, notice anything unusual, and explain your thinking in plain words. No AI tools yet — just your own eyes and judgment.

Systems are busy all day: people sign in, open files, run updates, and the system handles dozens of tasks in the background. Almost all of it is ordinary. Your real skill is noticing the few things that aren't.

Think like

A camera operator who spots one person moving against the crowd. A nurse who notices a single odd change on a monitor. A manager who sees movement in the store after closing.

Nothing is assumed — everything is observed.
