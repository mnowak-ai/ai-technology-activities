AI's strengths and blind spots in operations analysis
Pending

Read critically — not just quickly.

Before you paste and evaluate the AI output, understand where it tends to be strong and where it tends to slip. This shapes what you pay attention to.

AI often handles well

→Spotting volume patterns and workload spikes quickly.
→Identifying which tasks appear most repetitive across datasets.
→Generating plausible automation candidates from structured CSV data.
→Noting documentation gaps based on request frequency patterns.

Where AI tends to slip

!Recommending automation without knowing team capacity or readiness.
!Missing the human reasons behind patterns — why Monday spikes, not just that they do.
!Treating analysts as interchangeable — missing the individual workload stories.
!Sounding confident about things it cannot fully validate from data alone.
Keep these in mind as you review
