1Your tools

Three decisions, four criteria

You'll evaluate each documentation package against four criteria, then make one approval decision. Keep both in mind as you review.

Approve

Ready for organization-wide use. Every statement is accurate, uncertainty is honest, and another team can act on it.

Revise

The right information is there, but something needs fixing — a correction, a clarification, or a removal — before it goes out.

Reject

Too inaccurate, too unclear, or too risky to share. Sending this would do more harm than having no documentation.

Four evaluation criteria

ClarityCan someone understand this quickly, without extra explanation?
AccuracyDoes it reflect what actually happened — including what was uncertain?
UsefulnessCan someone act on this? Does it help prevent the same issue?
SimplicityIs it the shortest it can be without losing meaning?
