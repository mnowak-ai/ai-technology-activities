Sentinel has grown fast. New clients across healthcare, education, logistics, and public services have pushed workloads higher. Leadership suspects teams are spending too much time on repetitive tasks — but they need evidence before investing in new tools or systems.

Over the next five steps you'll read four operational reports, classify their data, find patterns, investigate specific workflows, and build a findings report. No AI tools yet — this phase is your own observation and analysis.

You are acting as an Operations Improvement Analyst. Your goal isn't to fix anything. It's to understand how work is being performed — and notice what the data reveals.
